# Introducing Horizon-Truth

Welcome to the official blog of **Horizon-Truth**. This platform is dedicated to tracking and verifying public claims, providing a reliable source of truth for the community.

<!-- truncate -->

## Our Organization: Horizon-Truth

Horizon-Truth is more than just a software project; it's a mission to bring transparency and accountability to public discourse. By leveraging community-driven verification and automated analysis engines, we aim to build a platform where facts are paramount.

## Our Development Partner: Dabbal Software Development PLC

The Horizon-Truth platform is proudly developed in partnership with **Dabbal Software Development PLC**. 

Dabbal Software Development PLC is a leading technology solutions provider specializing in building scalable, secure, and high-performance applications. Their expertise in modern web technologies (React, NestJS, Tailwind CCS) and cloud infrastructure has been instrumental in bringing the Horizon-Truth vision to life.

### Collaborative Engineering
The collaboration between Horizon-Truth and Dabbal Software focuses on:
- **Scalability**: Ensuring the platform can handle global data and traffic.
- **Security**: Implementing robust authentication and data protection mechanisms.
- **User Experience**: Designing intuitive interfaces for both contributors and consumers of truth.

## Join Us
As we continue to evolve, we invite you to explore our documentation and contribute to the mission. Together, we can build a more transparent future.
